This calculator uses: DO₂i = (Flow ÷ BSA) × CaO₂ × 10. Required flow = (Target DO₂i × BSA) ÷ (CaO₂ × 10).

Interpret with lactate trend, SvO₂/ScvO₂, perfusion pressure, and institutional flow limits rather than formula outputs alone.

▶ Methodology & clinical notes

Target strategy: Normothermic adult programs often target DO₂i at least 280–300 mL/min/m²; pediatric targets can be higher based on institutional policy.

Flow guidance: Required flow is an estimate from current Hb, SaO₂, PaO₂, and BSA. Clinical flow should still respect pressure, venous drainage, cannula, and surgical constraints.

Limitations: This model assumes steady-state mixing and does not account for rapid shifts in oxygen extraction, blood loss, retransfusion, or temperature effects.
