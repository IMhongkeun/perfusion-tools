▶ Methodology & clinical notes

Mosteller: BSA = √((Height(cm) × Weight(kg)) / 3600). Clinical default in many CPB workflows.

DuBois/Haycock/Boyd: Useful for sensitivity checks in pediatrics or extreme body habitus.

Clinical interpretation: Use indexed flow as guidance with lactate, venous saturation, temperature, and institutional protocols.
