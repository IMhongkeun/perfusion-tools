Input model: Select standard tubing inner diameter and provide length in cm, m, ft, or in.

Cylinder formula: V(mL) = (π/4) × ID(mm)2 × Length(m).

Interpretation: Output reflects tubing internal volume only and excludes connectors, reservoirs, oxygenator volume, and real-world tolerance effects.
